About

VAC is a clinic accompanying the medical needs of local hospitals, including University of Tennessee Medical Center, Blount Memorial Hospital and Turkey Creek Medical Center. Our goal at VAC is to provide immediate care for patients at their convenience. With our technology of scheduling online appointments with your medical needs beforehand, the capability to refer back to past appointments, and the ability to have access to your medical history makes our clinic different from our competitors.
